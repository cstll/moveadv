Additional Information

Do you want to work for your scenic views? So do we. On this trip, we take the shortest route up one mountain, and while it's short, that also makes it the steepest. So although we cover less distance, we accomplish more cardio.

We'll be doing work in circuits, which means we'll climb a bit of the mountain, stop to do strength training, take in the view, and take out the cameras. Then we repeat. Climb, training, pictures. You will see a total of 5 different, unique, amazing views on this trip. So bring your sense of adventure, and of course, your inner Champion.

Meeting Location: Front of the silver globe in Columbus Circle, New York.

Meeting Time: Please arrive at 8:45am to check in. The van leaves at 9am SHARP.

End Time: We will be back in the city by 2pm.

Things to Bring:

  • Comfortable weather appropriate clothing and comfortable, sturdy shoes. Hikings shoes are preferable.
  • Change of shirt and towel optional.
  • Gloves if you don't feel comfortable touching the dirt.
  • At least 1 Liter of water.
  • Sunscreen and/or bug spray if needed.
  • Snacks will be provided however we advise everyone to bring your own food.
  • Instructor contact info in case of emergencies.

It’s customary to tip the instructor if you enjoy your adventure.
