Fitness Chart - What trips are right for you?

Signature Fitness Trips

Let's Go Uphill - Approximately 3.5 mile hike with one major peak. Available in active and fit workout intensity levels. Intermediate technicality.

Multi Peak Sweat - 5-6 mile hike with strength exercises, completing atleast 2 major peaks. Available in FIT and ATHLETIC workout intensities. Intermediate technicality.

Circuit Climber - 2.5 mile hike with a series of circuits up the peak. Available in FIT and ATHLETIC workout intensities. Between beginner and intermediate technicality.

Stair Climb Bear Mountain - 3 mile hike with a strong stair climb to start it off with most of the elevation coming from stairs. Between views will be intense strength circuits. Available in ATHLETIC. Between beginner and intermediate technicality.

Power Up Bear Mountain - 3.5 mile hike with a few light scrambles up to Bear Mountain. Available in ATHLETIC. Advance technicality. 

Workout Intensity (fitness level needed to complete the fitness trip & workouts)

  • Active (beginner):  Suitable for people with average healthy lifestyles.
  • Fit (Intermediate):  Suitable for people who workout 1-3 times a week.
  • Athletic (Advanced):  Suitable for people who work out 2-5 times a week and are of above-average fitness level.

Technicality (hiking skill level needed to complete the trails use for the workout)

  • Beginner:  Suitable for people who have never hiked before; requires no prior experience.
  • Intermediate:  Suitable for people who have some prior experience hiking and outdoor terrain.
  • Advanced:  Suitable for people who have done outdoor hiking numerous times before and require little hiking instruction.
